Legal Framework & Privacy

1. Data Sovereignty Pledge

At AmanahNetwork, we treat your information as a sacred trust (Amanah). We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data, Aadhaar numbers, or contact information with any third-party marketers or external agencies.

2. Advanced Encryption

All sensitive data, including Aadhaar identification, is subjected to AES-256 bit encryption before being stored in our siloed database. This ensures that even in the event of unauthorized access, the raw data remains unreadable.

3. Legal Retention

We only retain information for the following purposes:

  • Compliance with financial auditing and anti-money laundering laws.
  • Verification of identity to prevent duplicate distribution fraud.
  • Official government inquiries where legally mandated.

4. Terms of Service

By using this platform, you agree that your contribution is voluntary and intended for direct poverty relief. AmanahNetwork reserves the right to refuse contributions that do not meet our verification standards.
